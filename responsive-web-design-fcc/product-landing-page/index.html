Who we are

ColArt wants to preserve and pass on our knowledge to future generations so that we never forget. As a company, we believe that culture has the power to keep communities alive.


ColArt was created to show our work to the rest of the world. Our products are hand-made by Colombian artisans who put all their passion, love, and knowledge into their work. Their techniques, a singular source of pride for these artisans, come from a unique blend of cultures that exists in our country, passed down from generation to generation over centuries.
